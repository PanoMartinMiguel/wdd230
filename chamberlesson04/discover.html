Make the most of your day with a short sightseeing tour of the city and a premium access pass to the Mazatlan Aquarium, which includes the Humboldt penguin exhibit and interactive tank areas. See educational exhibits like diving with sharks, sea lions, tropical and predatory birds, and more.

Deer Island is an Ecological Reserve that is located in the middle of the famous Three Islands of Mazatlan, you can reach it by jet ski, boat, sailboat or kayak where you can see different animals such as squirrels, badgers, bats, deer , among others.

Plaza Machado features French and Spanish-influenced architecture, and is one of the most attractive plazas in Mazatlan, as well as the main site of social activity in the historic center.

The Municipal Market of Mazatlán "Pino Suárez" was designed and built by Mr. Alejandro Loubet, built in iron and steel and based on the Art Nouveau style, using the same techniques that were used 7 years earlier in the construction of the Eiffel Tower . This new work marked the history of Mazatlán, placing it as the most progressive center of commerce and industry in the country.

The history of the name "Changueras" goes because when taking the fish to their wives, they passed the shellfish from one place to another with a monkey-shaped tool, from there they were baptized for eternity.
